About SeneX Holdings

We are a Sri Lankan engineering company delivering premium solar PV solutions for homes, businesses, and industry. Our qualified team of electrical, electronic, and mechanical specialists ensures safe, efficient, and long‑lasting systems.

  • Over 10 years of expertise in grid‑tied and off‑grid installations across all provinces.
  • 2MW+ installed annually with proven ROI and industry‑leading performance.
  • Premium international products selected for Sri Lankan climate conditions.
  • Qualified, certified team of multi‑disciplinary specialists.
  • Full‑service delivery: design, installation, maintenance & microgrid management.

Why Go Solar with SeneX?

  • Cut costs and stabilize your bills with clean generation.
  • Energy independence with hybrid/off‑grid options.
  • Environmental impact — reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Government incentives and net metering guidance.
  • Local support from our Nugegoda HQ and Matara branch.
